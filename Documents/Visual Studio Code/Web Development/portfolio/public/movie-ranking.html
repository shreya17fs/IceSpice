The Best Movies According to Asmita

My top 3 movies of all-time

To All The Boys I've Loved Before (series)

Rating : 🌟🌟🌟🌟

The film is a light hearted and fun watch. It was a very cute story about a girl named Lara Jean. She writes secret, soul-baring letters to her five crushes, but never meant to send them. Now they are out and wreaking havoc on her life.

3 idiots

Rating: 🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟

Two friends are searching for their long lost companion. They revisit their college days and recall the memories of their friend who inspired them to think differently.The movie is terrifically funny and engaging.

Kabhi Khushi Kabhi Ghum

Rating: 🌟🌟🌟🌟

This film shines from start to finish, and runs the gamut of emotions. The cinematography, sets, and costumes are brilliant. The sparkling performances of six of India's leading actors alone makes the movie worth seeing.
